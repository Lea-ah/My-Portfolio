DAYT

The Idea

PROBLEM:
When travelling, getting overwhelmed when looking for things to do. Too many websites to compare, usually the same 'Top 10' activities and ending up doing just basic tourist activities.


SOLUTION:  DAYT!
A mobile app to help you find what YOU like, based on your selected preferences (activities, budget, distance travelled, ...) just by swipping and adding to the calendar.
And all that curated by locals of the area.
Swipe, add to your calendar, drag & drop to organise your day and voila! It's a Dayt!
